Free In Home Consultation

We meet to discuss your ideas and plans for your project. We can provide the knowledge and expertise to assist you to visualize your ideas and formulate a budget.

Conceptual Design, Drawings, and Renderings

Turning your dream into reality begins with understanding what the end result may look like. We can help you with this by providing both concept plans and 3-Dimensional visuals.

Architectural Drawings and Building Permits

We can provide you with the necessary construction drawings to ensure things get built properly, and provide for any necessary permits required.

Full Construction and Project Management Services

Whether your project is a kitchen or bathroom renovation, basement completion, whole home renovation, addition or brand new construction, we can provide full general contracting services from start to finish.

Interior Design Services

Whether it is assisting with selecting material and colour choices or providing full interior design service, we are there to help you.

After Completion Services and Warranty

Completing your project is not the end of our relationship. We provide for a full 2 year warranty on all work we do and will be there should any service issues arise.
