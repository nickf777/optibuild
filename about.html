About Us

Located in Oakville, Ontario, Optibuild Design Inc. is a full service design/build contractor specializing in home renovations, home additions, and new home design projects in Oakville, Burlington, Mississauga and surrounding areas. With almost 3 decades of experience in the residential construction industry, you can be certain with Optibuild Design your home renovation project will be done right.

Our goal is to provide our clients with the best possible solution to meet and exceed their expectations.

Attention to detail, quality workmanship, superior materials, and courteous, professional service are what you can expect from the Optibuild Design team.

Through every step of the process, from concept to completion, we are there to assist you in reaching your home renovation goals in a timely and efficient manner.
